Venues

1. Wankhede Stadium, Mumbai

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra

Capacity: Approximately 33,000

Overview: One of the most famous cricket stadiums in India, the Wankhede Stadium is home to the Mumbai Indians (MI). Known for its vibrant atmosphere and electric crowd, it has hosted numerous memorable matches, including the IPL finals.

Notable Features: The stadium has a tradition of high-scoring games and thrilling finishes. It also boasts a stunning view of the Arabian Sea from its stands.

2. M. A. Chidambaram Stadium, Chennai

Location: Chennai, Tamil Nadu

Capacity: Approximately 50,000

Overview: Often referred to as Chepauk, this stadium is the fortress of the Chennai Super Kings (CSK). With its large capacity and passionate fans, it is known for its intimidating conditions for visiting teams.

Notable Features: The ground’s spin-friendly pitch and loyal fan base make it a challenging venue for opponents.

3. Eden Gardens, Kolkata

Location: Kolkata, West Bengal

Capacity: Approximately 66,000

Overview: Eden Gardens is one of the oldest and largest cricket stadiums in India. It serves as the home ground for the Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) and is renowned for its historic significance and passionate crowds.

Notable Features: The stadium’s large capacity and rich history make it a landmark venue for cricket enthusiasts.

4. M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bangalore

Location: Bangalore, Karnataka

Capacity: Approximately 40,000

Overview: This stadium is home to the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB). Known for its high-scoring games due to the flat pitch, it offers an exciting experience for both players and spectators.

Notable Features: The ground is famous for its excellent lighting and the enthusiastic support of its fans.

5. Arun Jaitley Stadium, Delhi

Location: Delhi

Capacity: Approximately 41,820

Overview: The Arun Jaitley Stadium, formerly known as Feroz Shah Kotla Ground, is the home ground of the Delhi Capitals (DC). The venue is known for its challenging pitch conditions and competitive matches.

Notable Features: The stadium has seen several dramatic matches and is renowned for its historic significance.

6. Sawai Mansingh Stadium, Jaipur

Location: Jaipur, Rajasthan

Capacity: Approximately 30,000

Overview: This stadium is the home ground of the Rajasthan Royals (RR). It is known for its unique pink-colored stands and a friendly pitch that supports both batting and bowling.

Notable Features: The ground’s vibrant atmosphere and enthusiastic crowd make it a memorable venue.

7. Punjab Cricket Association Stadium, Mohali

Location: Mohali, Punjab

Capacity: Approximately 26,000

Overview: The Punjab Cricket Association Stadium is the home of the Punjab Kings (PBKS). Known for its fast-paced pitch, it provides a balanced challenge for both batsmen and bowlers.

Notable Features: The stadium’s modern facilities and supportive crowd create an exciting match environment.

8. Sunrisers Hyderabad Stadium, Hyderabad

Location: Hyderabad, Telangana

Capacity: Approximately 55,000

Overview: The Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) play their home matches at this venue. Known for its large capacity and balanced pitch, it offers a great atmosphere for T20 cricket.

Notable Features: The stadium’s facilities and fan support contribute to a thrilling game experience.

9. Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad

Location: Ahmedabad, Gujarat

Capacity: Approximately 132,000

Overview: The Narendra Modi Stadium is the largest cricket stadium in the world and the home ground for the Gujarat Titans (GT). Its massive capacity and state-of-the-art facilities make it a premier venue for IPL matches.

Notable Features: The stadium’s size and modern amenities set it apart as a landmark venue for cricket.

10. Lucknow Stadium, Lucknow

Location: Lucknow, Uttar Pradesh

Capacity: Approximately 50,000

Overview: The new home ground for the Lucknow Super Giants (LSG), this stadium provides a fresh and exciting venue for IPL matches. Known for its modern infrastructure, it promises a great match experience.

Notable Features: The stadium’s design and facilities offer a contemporary cricketing environment.
